## Learning Objectives

After studying this directory, learners should be able to:

1. **Hearing Loss**
   - Differentiate conductive from sensorineural hearing loss
   - Perform and interpret tuning fork tests
   - Understand audiometry and imaging
   - Manage common causes of hearing loss

2. **Otitis Media**
   - Diagnose acute and chronic otitis media
   - Recognize complications requiring intervention
   - Apply appropriate antibiotic therapy
   - Know indications for tympanostomy tubes

3. **Sinusitis**
   - Distinguish viral from bacterial rhinosinusitis
   - Recognize complications of sinusitis
   - Apply appropriate medical management
   - Understand surgical options

4. **Pharyngitis and Tonsillitis**
   - Differentiate viral from bacterial pharyngitis
   - Apply Centor criteria appropriately
   - Recognize peritonsillar abscess
   - Know indications for tonsillectomy

5. **Head and Neck Masses**
   - Develop systematic differential diagnosis
   - Perform appropriate workup
   - Recognize malignancy warning signs
   - Understand management principles
