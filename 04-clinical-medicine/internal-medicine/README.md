## File Structure Template
Each file follows this comprehensive format:
```markdown
---
title: "[Topic Name]"
order: [Number]
category: "[Subspecialty]"
---

# [Topic Name]

## Overview
- Definition and scope
- Epidemiology
- Classification

## Pathophysiology
- Molecular/cellular mechanisms
- Disease progression
- Compensatory mechanisms

## Clinical Presentation
- Symptoms
- Physical examination findings
- Clinical subtypes

## Diagnosis
- Clinical assessment
- Laboratory studies
- Imaging modalities
- Diagnostic criteria

## Treatment
- Medical management
- Interventional procedures
- Surgical options
- Supportive care

## Complications
- Acute complications
- Chronic complications
- Management strategies

## Prognosis
- Natural history
- Prognostic factors
- Outcomes with treatment

## Key Points
- 5-10 essential takeaways

## References
- 10 key references (guidelines, trials, textbooks)
```

## Content Specifications
- **Length**: 500-700 lines per file
- **Format**: Markdown with YAML frontmatter
- **Depth**: Medical student/resident level
- **Citations**: Evidence-based, current guidelines
- **Quality**: Comprehensive, clinically relevant

## Usage
Files are organized by subspecialty for easy navigation. Each subdirectory is self-contained with an overview (claude.md) and 4 topic-specific files covering the most important conditions in that field.
